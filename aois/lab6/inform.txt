island - any piece of sub-continental land is surrounded by water
peninsula - a body of land surrounded by water on three sides
gulf - a portion of the ocean that penetrates land
cape - a high point of land that extends into a river lake or ocean
relief - the highest and lowest elevation points in an area
aquifer - an underground reservoir of water which can be extracted for surface use
archipelago - a chain or set of islands grouped together
biosphere - the plant and animal life on the earth
climate - the long term trends in weather conditions for an area
density - the number of items per unit area such as persons per square kilometre
equator - zero degrees latitude divides the earth into the Northern and Southern hemispheres
meridian - line of longitude
region - an area which is marked common characteristics
weather - the short term atmospheric conditions
scale - relationship between distance on a map and on the earths surface
